Grade 6 Mathematics Formulas

1. Number and Operations

Ratios and Proportions

Ratio: $a:b$

Proportion: $\frac{a}{b} = \frac{c}{d}$

Percentages

Percentage: $\text{Part} \div \text{Whole} \times 100\%$

Percentage to decimal: $\text{Percentage} \div 100$

Decimal to percentage: $\text{Decimal} \times 100\%$

2. Algebra

Simple Equations

$x + a = b$

$x - a = b$

$ax = b$

Simple Inequalities

$x < a$, $x > a$, $x \leq a$, $x \geq a$

3. Geometry

Perimeter

Rectangle: $P = 2l + 2w$

Square: $P = 4s$

Area

Rectangle: $A = l \times w$

Square: $A = s^2$

Triangle: $A = \frac{1}{2} \times b \times h$

Parallelogram: $A = b \times h$

Circles

Circumference: $C = \pi d$ or $C = 2\pi r$

Area: $A = \pi r^2$

Volume

Rectangular prism: $V = l \times w \times h$

4. Measurement

Speed

$\text{Speed} = \frac{\text{Distance}}{\text{Time}}$

Density

$\text{Density} = \frac{\text{Mass}}{\text{Volume}}$

5. Data and Statistics

Mean (Average)

$\text{Mean} = \frac{\text{Sum of all values}}{\text{Number of values}}$

Range

$\text{Range} = \text{Highest value} - \text{Lowest value}$

6. Angles

Sum of angles in a triangle: $180°$

Sum of angles on a straight line: $180°$

Sum of angles around a point: $360°$

7. Transformations

Translation: $(x, y) \rightarrow (x + a, y + b)$

Reflection: $(x, y) \rightarrow (-x, y)$ or $(x, -y)$ depending on axis

8. Simple Interest

$I = P \times R \times T$

Where:

  • $I$ = Interest
  • $P$ = Principal
  • $R$ = Rate (as a decimal)
  • $T$ = Time
